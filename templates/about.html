Our
Mission

Our mission here at Cognosphere is to provide students K-8 with free tech education — introducing and providing them with the fundamentals needed to succeed later on in life. We believe it shouldn’t matter how rich or poor a person is; everyone deserves the opportunity to learn, grow, and prepare for the future that lies before them. The further we develop and advance as a society, the more essential technology becomes. Cognosphere wants to help students stay ahead of the curve with our cost-free, instructional programs.

Cognosphere is a variant of the word cognoscere, which means "to learn" in latin. Adding the -sphere suffix emphasizes our goal to make quality tech education available to everyone around the world!
